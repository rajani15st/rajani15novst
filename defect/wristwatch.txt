Negative Test cases - Wrist Watch
**********************************************************************************
1) In the case of an analog watch, check the correctness time displayed by the second, minute, and hour hand 
    of the watch.
2) In the case of a digital watch, check the digital display for hours, minutes, and seconds is correctly displayed.
3) Check if the watch is waterproof or not.
4) Verify that the numbers in the dial are clearly visible or not.
5) Check if the watch is having a date and day display or not.
6) Verify that clock’s time can be corrected using the key in case of an analog clock and buttons in case of a 
     digital clock.
7) Check if the second hand of the watch makes ticking sound or not.
8) Verify if the brand of the watch and check if its visible in the dial.
9) Check if the clock is having stopwatch, timers, and alarm functionality or not.
10) Verify if the watch comes with any guarantee or warranty.
11) Verify if the dial has glass covering or plastic, check if the material is breakable or not.
12) Verify if the dial’s glass/plastic is resistant to minor scratches or not.
13) Varify belt or chain used is comfortable or not and it’s length
14) Check if the watch properly fits on the wrist or not.
15) Check is all accessories of the Watch are fitted correctly.
16) Check if the Watch is working without power or a power cell.
17) The check can a user increase and decrease the size of the watch strap or chain.
18) Check if the watch is working or not when the second needle is not working.
19) Check if the GPS of the watch is working without a sim and internet.
20) To verify that Watch is not making any sound when it’s in the movement.
21) To verify that the Watch is working at different climate environmental conditions.
